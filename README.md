<h2><a name="TOC-Biography"></a><span style="font-family:Georgia;background-color:transparent"><font size="4">Biography</font></span></h2>
<p><font face="georgia, serif" size="3"><span style="line-height:106%">
	I</span> </font><span style="background-color:transparent"><font face="georgia, serif" size="3">got my Ph.D. degree from </font></span><span style="font-family:georgia,serif;font-size:medium;line-height:16.96px"> </span><a href="http://math.dlut.edu.cn/" style="font-family:georgia,serif;font-size:medium;line-height:16.96px" rel="nofollow">School of Mathematical Sciences</a><span style="font-family:georgia,serif;font-size:medium;line-height:16.96px">, </span><a href="http://www.dlut.edu.cn/" style="font-family:georgia,serif;font-size:medium;line-height:16.96px" rel="nofollow">Dalian University of Technology</a><span style="background-color:transparent"><font face="georgia, serif" size="3"> in 2017. Before that, I </font></span><font face="georgia, serif" size="3" style="background-color:transparent"><span style="line-height:106%">was a joint-training Ph.D. student in </span><a href="http://math.dlut.edu.cn/" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">School of Mathematical Sciences</a><span style="line-height:106%">, </span><a href="http://www.dlut.edu.cn/" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">Dalian University of Technology</a><span style="line-height:106%"> and </span><a href="http://eecs.ucmerced.edu/" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">Electrical Engineering and Computer Science</a><span style="line-height:106%">, </span><a href="http://www.ucmerced.edu/" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">University of California at Merced</a><span style="line-height:106%">, directed by Profs. </span><span style="line-height:1.5;background-color:transparent"> </span><a href="http://cggi.dlut.edu.cn/?page_id=221&amp;lang=en" style="line-height:1.5;background-color:transparent" rel="nofollow">Zhixun Su</a><span style="line-height:1.5;background-color:transparent"> and </span><a href="http://faculty.ucmerced.edu/mhyang/" style="line-height:1.5;background-color:transparent" rel="nofollow">Ming-Hsuan Yang</a><span style="background-color:transparent"><span style="line-height:1.5">. I was a visiting Ph.D. student in </span><a href="http://vcg.seas.harvard.edu/" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">Visual Computing Group</a> at<span style="line-height:1.5"> <a href="http://www.harvard.edu/" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">Harvard University</a>, working with Prof. </span></span></font><span style="background-color:transparent;line-height:24px"><font face="georgia, serif" size="3"><a href="http://vcg.seas.harvard.edu/people/hanspeter-pfister" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">Hanspeter Pfister</a> and Dr. <a href="http://people.seas.harvard.edu/~dqsun/" style="line-height:106%" rel="nofollow">Deqing Sun</a> in 2015.</font></span></p>
<p><font face="georgia, serif" size="3"><span style="line-height:106%"> 
My research interest includes image deblurring, image/video analysis and enhancement, and related vision problems. </span></font></p>
